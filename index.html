Bio

I recently graduated from Michigan State University. I majored in Media and Information with a concentration in Social and Interactive Media and a Minor in Information Technology.
Yeah, I know... quite the mouthful.

During my stay at MSU I dipped my feet into a few professional web development experience. I got my first gig at SNworks, a partner company of The State News. There, a handful of other students and myself designed and built responsive websites for newspapers from major universities (including MSU's) using our in-house Content Management System called Gryphon. We maintained our sites (roughly 30) through Agile Workflow using a ticket-based system (JIRA).

During my senior year I worked at Jackson National Life, a top-ranked US financial services company based in Lansing. In my time there, I assisted in the following: maintaining and updating websites for several of Jackson's affiliated companies, establishing customized style guides for specific projects, researching industry-related web standards, producing internal referencing documents, and creating wireframes/mock-ups for supplementary content.

Now graduated, I am currently employed at Perficient Digital as a UI Engineer.
